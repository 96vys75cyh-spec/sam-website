What this website collects

This website does not use analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising cookies. The only personal data we receive is what you choose to send us — for example, if you email us or use a "Start a conversation" link, we receive your name, email address, and the content of your message.

How we use it

We use this information only to respond to your enquiry, and to follow up on any resulting conversation about research, pilot, or licensing collaboration. We do not sell or share it with third parties.

How long we keep it

We retain enquiry correspondence for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to and follow up on it.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to ask what personal data we hold about you, and to ask us to correct or delete it. To do so, contact info@smartaccessandmonitoring.co.uk.
